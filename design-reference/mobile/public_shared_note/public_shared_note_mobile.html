Finding Peace in the Stillness

Psalms 46:10 Meditation

"Be still, and know that I am God..."

Today's world moves at a pace that often feels designed to drown out the internal whisper of the spirit. I found myself sitting by the old oak tree this morning, watching the fog lift off the valley floor.

It occurred to me that stillness isn't the absence of motion, but the presence of focus. In the quiet, the patterns of grace become visible. We are not called to be busy; we are called to be faithful.

When we stop striving, we begin seeing. The gold in the palette of our lives isn't found in the highlight reels, but in the subtle gradients of daily devotion.
