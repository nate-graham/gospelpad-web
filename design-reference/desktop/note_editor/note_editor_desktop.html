Personal Reflection
Psalm 23:1 close
Isaiah 40:31 close

I found myself returning to the words of the Shepherd's Psalm this morning. There is a specific kind of peace that comes when the world is quiet, before the digital noise begins to crowd the mind.

When we read Psalm 23:1, the emphasis is often on the lack of want. But perhaps the true sanctuary is found in the "He leads me." It is an active surrendering of the path.

"Even though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I will fear no evil..."
