Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Your journey is unique and your needs may change. You can upgrade or downgrade your access at any time from your account settings.

Is there a discount for ministries?

We offer special considerations for smaller missionary teams and registered non-profits. Please reach out to our Curators for a custom arrangement.

What happens to my notes if I cancel?

Your data remains yours. If you downgrade, your journals remain accessible for reading, though creation of new entries may be limited based on the free tier.
